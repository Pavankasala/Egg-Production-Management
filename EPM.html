What is Egg Production ?

Egg production refers to the process of raising hens (female chickens) primarily for the purpose of producing eggs for human consumption. This process involves managing the environment, feeding, and health of the hens to ensure a consistent and safe supply of eggs.

Vital Roles of Egg Production:

  • Nutritional Value: Eggs are an inexpensive yet rich source of high-quality protein, vitamins, and minerals.
  • Economic Importance: For many farmers, egg production is a key income source.
  • Sustainability: Compared to other protein sources like meat, egg production is relatively efficient.

Egg Production Process

Breeding

Hens are selectively bred for high-quality egg production.

Feeding

Hens are fed a nutritious diet essential for strong eggshells.

Housing

Providing comfortable and safe housing for hens.

Collection

Regular collection of eggs to ensure freshness.

Management and Best Practices

Successful egg production requires careful management practices including:

  • Regular health checks of hens.
  • Maintaining a clean and safe environment.
  • Providing a balanced diet to meet nutritional needs.
  • Monitoring egg production for quality and quantity.
